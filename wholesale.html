How It Works

Flower Availability

We offer a range of fresh Indian flowers subject to seasonal availability and import conditions. Availability may vary depending on seasonal production, import schedules, market conditions, and biosecurity clearance requirements. Customers are encouraged to place orders early during peak festival and wedding seasons.

Minimum Order Quantities

Minimum order quantities (MOQs) may apply for certain wholesale and bulk orders depending on flower variety, delivery location, packaging requirements, and event size. Our team will provide customised quotations based on your requirements.

Pricing & Quotations

Wholesale pricing is determined based on flower type, quantity ordered, seasonal availability, delivery requirements, and packaging specifications. Prices are subject to change without prior notice. Formal quotations may be provided upon request.

Ordering Process

To place a wholesale or bulk enquiry, please provide your flower requirements, estimated quantity, preferred delivery date, delivery location, and event or business details. Our team will respond with availability, pricing, and delivery options.

Quality & Freshness

We strive to maintain high standards of freshness and quality. As flowers are natural and perishable products, slight variations in colour, fragrance, size, and bloom stage may occur. We work closely with sourcing partners and logistics providers to ensure flowers arrive in the best possible condition.

Cancellations & Claims

Wholesale order cancellations or modifications may only be accepted before processing and procurement begins. Customers must inspect products upon delivery and report any concerns within 24 hours with supporting photographs.
